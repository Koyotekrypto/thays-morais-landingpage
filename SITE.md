# CONECTADEV Site Structure

## Overview
A unified, high-conversion single-page landing page (SPA) built with React and TailwindCSS. The page serves as a premium portfolio and lead generation tool for the CONECTADEV agency.

## Sections (Top to Bottom)
1. **Navigation (Navbar):** Sticky header with logo, primary links, and CTA.
2. **Hero:** High-impact value proposition, main CTA, and trust signals (logos).
3. **Services (ServiceMatrix):** 4 core pillars of offerings.
4. **Methodology (OperationalFlow):** Visual explanation of the process.
5. **Success Cases (ValidationLog):** Social proof and metrics.
6. **Conversion (Lead Form):** Final CTA and contact form.
7. **Footer:** Legal links, branding, and contact info.
