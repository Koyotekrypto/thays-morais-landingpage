# CONECTADEV Design System

## Atmosphere
Boutique Tech: Professional, trustworthy, futuristic but accessible. Focused on precision, clarity, and premium feel.

## Colors
- **Target Audience:** SMEs (PMEs) in Brazil looking for digital transformation
- **Navy (Backgrounds & Surfaces):**
  - Background: `#0D1B2A` (Deep Navy)
  - Panel/Surface: `#1B2838` (Midnight Panel) 
  - Elevated Surface: `#243447`
  - Border: `#2D4052`
  - Dimmed Elements: `#718096`
  - Text: `#E0E1DD` (Silver Steel)
- **Primary Accent (Cyan):**
  - Main Accent: `#00B4D8` (Electric Cyan)
  - Hover/Focus: `#0096C7` (Sky Blue)
  - Highlight: `#48CAE4`

## Typography
- **Display/Headings:** Montserrat (Clean, modern, geometric)
- **Body/UI:** Inter (Highly readable, neutral)

## Geometry & Effects
- **Corners:** Generously rounded (`16px` / `rounded-2xl` for large elements, `8px` / `rounded-lg` for smaller ones).
- **Glassmorphism:** Subtle background blur (`backdrop-blur-md` or `10px`), with semi-transparent navy backgrounds (`rgba(27, 40, 56, 0.6)`).
- **Shadows & Glows:** Soft cyan glows for interactivity (`0 0 20px rgba(0, 180, 216, 0.15)`).

## Visual Identity
Eliminate standard "hacker/terminal" tropes (scanlines, heavy grids, aggressive magenta). Introduce clean, readable panels with luminous borders and generous padding to signify high-end service quality.
